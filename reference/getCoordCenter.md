# Get Coord Center

Get center of rectangle defined by upper left and lower right
coordinates

## Usage

``` r
getCoordCenter(upperLeftLat, upperLeftLong, lowerRightLat, lowerRightLong)
```

## Arguments

- upperLeftLat:

  (numeric) latitude of upper left point

- upperLeftLong:

  (numeric) longitude of upper left point

- lowerRightLat:

  (numeric) latitude of lower right point

- lowerRightLong:

  (numeric) longitude of lower right point
